**Stay Safe Online – Checklist for College Students (India)**

✅ Use strong, unique passwords for every account (at least 12 characters with symbols and numbers).

✅ Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) on email, social media, and banking apps.

✅ Never click on suspicious links or attachments received via email, SMS, or WhatsApp.

✅ Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking, UPI payments, or logging into sensitive accounts.

✅ Keep your devices updated with the latest security patches and antivirus software.

✅ Do not overshare personal information (phone number, address, college ID) on social media.

✅ Verify job/internship offers before sharing documents—many scams target students.

💰 UPI \& Financial Safety Tips

💳 Always verify the receiver’s name before confirming any UPI payment.

💳 Never approve “Collect Requests” unless you are sure—scammers often misuse this feature.

💳 Do not share OTP, UPI PIN, or banking details with anyone—even if they claim to be from a bank.

🚨 If You Face Cyber Fraud

Report immediately at: https://cybercrime.gov.in

Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930
